Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to use a VR headset?

No. You can run World.io normally on your computer.



How do I create an account?

After downloading the software, you will need to use your crypto credentials to set up your user account. To get credentials, you'll need a wallet. We recommend MetaMask.



What blockchain does World.io use?

World.io runs on the Eva blockchain, a carbon-neutral solution to keeping nodes in sync.



How does World.io make money?

We collect transaction fees from many of the global brands rapidly joining the platform as virtual vendors. We also rely on sponsors and donations.



What is Eva and how much does it cost?

Eva is the native cryptocurrency. If you prefer to use another cryptocurrency, Eva will act as a proxy and automate any necessary conversions. You can track the price of Eva on Coinbase.



Are there any rules or content restrictions?

Yes. Please refer to the Code of Conduct and Content Policy. Due to the decentralized architecture, policies are enforced with artificial intelligence embedded in the platform.



Can I use World.io if I'm not a developer?

Yes. The majority of users are not developers. Non-developers take advantage of a vibrant ecosystem of intuitive apps to accomplish various tasks in the world.
